When contributing to IndieWeb, you should either:
- Try to fix problems you find with any website that is not in city (city is for user websites)
- Or try to make a website for city.

If you are making a city, for the purpouses of the database, please start it with a letter to make it more sortable.
Keep it lowercase, and if you wish to seperate words, use a dash: -

You may also fix a city website if you are the owner of that website.
database.html may or may not exist when you are reading this file. However, if it does exist, please
add your city to the database so it can be searched in the alphabetical order.

When adding your city to the database, you may optionally add a description to the city.
Please keep cities in alphabetical order. Assume dashes come before A in alphabetical order.

Also, please keep all content contributed appropriate for most ages. This is vauge, and will be determined
at the discretion of whoever accepts the pull request. However, if you want rules, consider reading the
code of conduct.

If you add a website to IndieWeb, keep in mind IndieWeb is technically public domain, and by contributing,
you agree to make your website public domain when you add it, as it becomes part of IndieWeb.
